Discipline Tracker

Discipline,
made visible.

Forge is a self-mastery discipline tracker that brings habits, workouts, steps, mood, and consistency into one daily behaviour system. It turns the actions you take into measurable signals — so you can see whether you are living by your standards, day after day.

Workouts & Steps

Log workouts and daily movement. Forge measures whether you showed up for the training you committed to — turning physical effort into a clear record of follow-through, not just activity.

Habits & Standards

Track the daily habits that define you — the ones you complete and the ones you resist. Forge turns each one into proof of whether your actions match the standards you set for yourself.

Mood & Consistency

Log how you feel alongside what you do. Over time, the connection between your daily routines and your state shows up clearly — a steady, honest record of follow-through rather than isolated entries.

Discipline

Your standards,
scored daily.

Habits, workouts, and movement combined into one daily discipline score — a clear, daily measure of how closely your actions are matching your standards.
