Privacy Policy

This policy describes how 4BEES HEALTH LIMITED collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Forge app. By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. Effective date: 27 February 2026.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Forge uses a local-first data model. The following data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers:

  • Habit tracking data
  • Exercise and step tracking data
  • Mood and behavioural inputs
  • Performance scores and analytics
  • App usage preferences

B. Account Information

If you create an account, we collect your email address and encrypted (hashed) authentication credentials. Authentication data is hashed before transmission and stored securely via Supabase infrastructure. We do not store plain-text passwords.

C. Health Data

If you grant permission, Forge may access step count data through Apple HealthKit. Health data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

D. Diagnostic Data

We may collect limited diagnostic information such as crash logs and performance analytics to improve app stability.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Authenticate user accounts
  • Improve functionality and stability
  • Enable subscription features
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse

We do not sell personal data. We do not use data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Behavioural tracking data remains stored locally on your device. Account authentication data is securely stored using industry-standard encryption and hashing methods. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

4. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time using the in-app "Delete Account" feature. When you delete your account:

  • Authentication records are permanently removed from our backend database
  • Local data remains on your device until the app is uninstalled
  • Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data unless backed up by your device system

No manual contact is required to delete your account.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

All subscription payments are processed through Apple's in-app purchase system. We do not collect or store payment information. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled through your Apple ID settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Forge is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Third-Party Services

We use secure backend infrastructure (Supabase) solely for authentication storage. We do not share behavioural tracking data with third parties.

8. Data Retention

Authentication data is retained only while your account is active. Upon deletion of your account, authentication data is permanently removed.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your account information
  • Delete your account at any time within the app
  • Withdraw HealthKit permissions through your device settings

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of any updates.
